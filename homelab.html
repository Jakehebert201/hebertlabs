Homelab

A Debian-based homelab focused on media, storage, and practical self-hosted infrastructure.

This lab runs on a Debian server and centers around a Servarr stack with Gluetun, local NAS storage, and Plex. It is where I practice the kind of systems work I actually enjoy: Linux administration, service reliability, routing, storage management, and keeping real workloads online.

Public traffic for this site is handled separately through a VPS, Cloudflare, and Nginx, while the homelab focuses on internal services, media, and infrastructure practice.

Overview

What this homelab is doing

This homelab is built around one main Debian server that handles media and storage responsibilities while also serving as a practical place to build Linux, container, and networking experience. It is less about theoretical infrastructure and more about running useful services that have to stay available and organized.
